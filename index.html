How it works

Three messages. One smooth ride.

No apps to download, no accounts, no payment up front. Just chat with a real human on WhatsApp.

1

Text us your trip

Send a WhatsApp message with your pickup location, drop-off, how many passengers, and when you need to leave.

2

Get car & price

Our operator confirms the right car for your group and sends you a clear, fixed price — plus driver name and plate number.

3

Sit back & ride

Your car arrives at your door. Pay the driver in cash or by transfer when you arrive — whichever works for you.
